## System Rules

### Quiz Mechanics
- **Trigger**: Say "quiz me" or similar
- **Format**: Russian word → translate to English
- **Input**: Type answers directly in chat
- **Quiz Size**: 10 words per session
- **Selection**: Mix of new words + review (relatively random)
- **Review Policy**: Missed words return to pool

### Progression System

**Level Progression:**
1. **A0 Words** → graduate (85% accuracy + 50% coverage)
2. **A0 Phrases** (using A0 words) → graduate (85% accuracy + 50% coverage)
3. **A1 Words** → graduate (85% accuracy + 50% coverage)
4. **A1 Phrases** (using A1 words) → graduate (85% accuracy + 50% coverage)
5. Continue pattern: A2 words → A2 phrases → B1 words → B1 phrases → etc.

**Current Phase:** A0 Words

**Graduation Criteria (applies to all phases):**
- 85% all-time accuracy
- 100 words gotten correct at least once

**Word Mastery:**
- ○ Not Yet Seen → ↻ In Review (after first quiz)
- ↻ In Review → ✓ Mastered (after 3+ correct)
- Missed words stay in rotation until mastered

### After Each Quiz
Claude will automatically:
1. Check answers and provide immediate feedback
2. Update vocabulary table (status, counts, dates)
3. Update Current Progress section (%, accuracy, stats)
4. Add entry to Quiz History
5. Show summary stats + detailed breakdown
